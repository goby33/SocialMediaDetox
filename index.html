SocialMediaDetox

Our society is increasingly connected to social networks, such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat. While these platforms offer many benefits, such as instant communication and easy access to information, they also have their downsides. In recent years, studies have shown that social media can have negative effects on mental health, self-esteem, and personal relationships. As concerned citizens, we have created this website to share our insights and observations on the potential harm of social media. We hope to raise awareness of the risks associated with excessive use of these platforms and encourage responsible online behavior. We believe that by working together, we can create a safer and healthier digital environment for everyone.

Who we are ?

We are five computer science students who are passionate about technology and aware of the harmful effects of social media on mental health and well-being. As young adults, we have noticed an increase in excessive screen time among young people, which has motivated us to take action. Therefore, we have decided to create this site to raise awareness about the negative effects of social media.

As computer science students, we have a deep understanding of technology and its impact on society. We know how social media works, how it is designed to manipulate users, and how it can have a negative impact on mental health.

Our goal is to educate people about responsible use of social media and to help them become aware of potential risks associated with excessive use. We are here to assist you and answer your questions.
