Advices to keep yourself safe

Here are some steps you can take to protect yourself from the downsides of Twitter:

If you are being harassed or cyber bullied you can report the accounts doing so, block them and set your account temporarily private or set replies to your tweets to “followers only”

To overcome the limit of 280 characters you can either subscribe to Twitter Blue (the limit is 4000 characters) or reply to your own tweet and make a thread.

About privacy, you can set your account private, set the privacy of your tweets to predefined circles of your followers.

To avoid someone else logging in your twitter account you can pay for Twitter Blue and enable two factor authentification.
