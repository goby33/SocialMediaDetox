Five dangers you should consider before or while using Snapchat

1. A false sense of security

Many people have a false sense of security when using Snapchat. They are convinced that the messages they send disappear and that there are no more traces. But the real danger is that they can share embarrassing videos or photos and the receiver can save them using other apps or devices. That's why it's very important to be more careful about the content we share on Snapchat. Especially for teenagers. Parents can be made aware and informed to have a discussion with their children and talk to them about the consequences this can bring.

2. Cyberbullying

Cyber bullying or cyber harassment is a common phenomenon on all social media that everyone should be aware of. What kind of harassment do people experience on Snapchat? Knowing that Snapchat allows its users to send ephemeral messages, stalkers use Snapchat to harass people without leaving a trace. Thus, victims, even if they decide to file a complaint, have no evidence.

3. Easy connection with strangers and the dangers of Snapchat

With Snapchat, you can connect with anyone, without any control. Knowing that you can share your location with your network, it is very dangerous to use this feature. Your entire network, even people you don't know, can have access to your location in real time. The danger, as it has already happened, is that in case of a conflict, everyone in your network knows where you live.

4. Screen time addiction - gamifying time spent on the app with Snapstreaks

As we saw earlier, Snapstreaks encourage users to log in every day and share snaps with their friends every day. Snapstreaks can get people addicted to Snapchat and create long-term stress that is hard to get rid of.

5. Mental health - creating unrealistic image expectations with filters and selfie edits

Snapchat is the source of the famous unrealistic lenses and filters. Many users became addicted to these filters, as some could no longer accept their real faces. Many resorted to plastic surgery to finally look like their filters or just look like the celebrities who use filters.
